# Executive Overview

## What is RareRupees?
RareRupees is a specialized, full-stack web application designed for numismatists and hobbyists to catalog, track, and manage collections of unique Indian coins. It provides a digital ledger for physical coin collections, offering a visually rich and intuitive interface to browse, filter, and organize coin inventories.

## The Problem
Collectors often struggle with:
- **Disorganized Inventories**: Relying on spreadsheets or physical notebooks.
- **Lack of Visuals**: Inability to easily view the condition and details of coins remotely.
- **Difficulty in Valuation**: Hard to track specific mint marks, years, and conditions that drive value.
- **Accessibility**: No easy way to share or view collections on the go.

## The Solution
RareRupees solves these problems by providing:
- **Centralized Digital Catalog**: A secure, cloud-based database for all coin entries.
- **Rich Visual Gallery**: High-quality image uploads for front and rear views of every coin.
- **Smart Filtering**: Instant sorting by denomination, year, mint, and condition.
- **Specialized Categories**: Dedicated support for standard denominations (₹1, ₹2, ₹5, ₹10, ₹20) and special commemorative coins.
- **Secure Access**: Role-based authentication ensuring only authorized admins can modify the catalog.

## Target Audience
- **Serious Numismatists**: Collectors focusing on Indian currency history.
- **Hobbyist Collectors**: Individuals organizing personal collections.
- **Coin Dealers**: Professionals needing a digital showcase for their inventory.
